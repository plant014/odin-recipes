The Perfect Sushi Rice

Description

Here is a recipe for the perfect sushi rice. You can eat this alone or roll into your favorite sushi

roll with ingredients of choice. I use strips of carrots, cucumbers and slices of avacado. You can adjust

the amount of vinegar in this recipe to suit your taste

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 20 mins

Total: 25 mins

Servings: 15

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked glutinous white rice (sushi rice)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

Step 1

  1. Rinse the rice in a strainer or colander until the water runs clear.
  2. Combine with water in a medium saucepan.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover and cook for 20 mins.
  4. Rice should be tender and water should be absorbed.
  5. Cool until cool enough to handle

Step 2

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the rice vinegar, oil, sugar and salt
  2. Cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Cool, then stir into the cooked rice. When you pour this into the rice it will seem wet.
  4. Keep stirring and the rice will dry as it cools.
